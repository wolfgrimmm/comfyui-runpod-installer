    <section id="limitations">
        <div class="title" style="font-family: 'lubri'; font-size: 1.6em; color: #fff;">
          Limitations
        </div>
      
        <div class="content">
          <div class="description">
            All models have limits, including <span class="highlight">Ovi</span> 
          </div>
      
          <ul class="description" style="line-height: 1.8; margin-top: 20px; margin-left: 20px;">
            <li style="margin-bottom: 15px;">
              <strong>Video branch constraints.</strong>
              Visual quality inherits from the pretrained <em>WAN 2.2 5B ti2v</em> backbone. 
            </li>
      
            <li style="margin-bottom: 15px;">
              <strong>Speed/memory vs. fine detail.</strong>
              The <em>11B parameter</em> model (<em>5B visual + 5B audio + 1B fusion</em>) and high spatial compression rate balance inference speed and memory, 
              limiting extremely fine-grained details, tiny objects, or intricate textures in complex scenes.
            </li>
      
            <li style="margin-bottom: 15px;">
              <strong>Human-centric bias.</strong>
              Data skews toward human-centric content, so Ovi performs best on human-focused scenarios. The audio branch enables highly emotional, dramatic short clips within this focus.
            </li>
      
            <li style="margin-bottom: 15px;">
              <strong>Pretraining only stage.</strong>
              Without extensive post-training or RL stages, outputs vary more between runs. 
              <em>Tip:</em> Try multiple random seeds for better results.
            </li>
          </ul>
        </div>
      </section>
